Color system reference

Logo-led calm, with coral energy.

This reference records the selected WeaveMark UI palette: logo-true teal and navy as the identity system, plus coral spark as a sparse complementary accent for emphasis, delight, and clearer hierarchy.

Suggested ratio

Keep the identity calm and logo-led: about 75% neutral surface, 20% navy/teal/cyan identity accents, and 5% coral spark.

Selected direction, plus alternatives.

Coral spark is the production choice. The alternatives remain here as future reference.

Coral spark

Warm, energetic, and naturally complementary to cyan without feeling like an error state.

Practical takeaways.

The complement should be a spice, not a second brand palette.

Best default

Coral spark feels friendly, public-release ready, and naturally balances the cyan logo tones.

Best for evidence notes

Golden insight is strong for analytical pages, but it may conflict with warning semantics.

Best for language concepts

Orchid works for formal specification and Processor ideas, but should not dominate the general UI.

Use with restraint

Rose is memorable, but should stay rare because it approaches alert and error territory.

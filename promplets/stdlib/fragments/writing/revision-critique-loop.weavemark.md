# Revision Critique Loop

@note
  Reusable writing layer for improving a document through multiple critique and
  revision passes.

Use this layer when writing quality should become observable and iteratively
improvable.

## Revision obligations

- Define the evaluation criteria before critiquing.
- Critique structure, argument, evidence, voice, specificity, reader friction,
  and actionability separately.
- Distinguish fatal issues from polish issues.
- Require examples from the draft when identifying a problem.
- Offer at least one conservative and one ambitious revision path when tradeoffs
  are real.
- Preserve the author's intent unless explicitly changing it.
- End with a checklist the author can use for a final pass.

## Required critique shape

When applicable, include:

1. **Quality bar** - what a good document must accomplish.
2. **Issue table** - issue, evidence, impact, fix, and tradeoff.
3. **Revision plan** - ordered changes from structural to local.
4. **Rewrite samples** - only where a sample resolves ambiguity.
5. **Decision points** - places where the author must choose direction.
6. **Final-check list** - small checks before sending or publishing.

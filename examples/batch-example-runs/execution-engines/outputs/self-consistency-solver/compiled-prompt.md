## Problem

A workshop sells 18 starter kits in week 1. Sales increase by 6 kits each week for 10 weeks total. Each kit generates $37 in profit before fixed costs, and the workshop pays a one-time $1,250 tooling cost. What is the total profit after 10 weeks?

## Instructions

Work through the problem one logical step at a time:
- State what you are calculating and why at each step.
- Show the arithmetic or reasoning clearly.
- Complete all steps before giving the final answer.

## Output Format

Provide your answer in this format:
1. **Working**: Show each step of your calculation.
2. **Answer**: State the final numerical answer clearly on its own line, prefixed with "ANSWER: " (number only, no units or explanation).

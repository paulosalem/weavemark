## Problem

A workshop sells 18 starter kits in week 1. Sales increase by 6 kits each week for 10 weeks total. Each kit generates $37 in profit before fixed costs, and the workshop pays a one-time $1,250 tooling cost. What is the total profit after 10 weeks?

## Instructions

- State what you are calculating and why at each step.
- Show the arithmetic clearly.
- Check that the 10-week sales pattern, per-kit profit, and one-time tooling cost are all accounted for.
- After completing all steps, state the final answer clearly on its own line.

## Required Output Format

Provide your answer in this format:
1. **Working**: Show each step of your calculation.
2. **Answer**: State the final numerical answer on its own line, prefixed exactly with "ANSWER: " and followed by the number only, with no units or explanation.
